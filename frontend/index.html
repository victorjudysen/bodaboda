What we built

A ride-hailing platform for boda boda trips, developed as part of CS 421

Registered riders

Riders sign up with their bike plate and contact number. Customers can see who is available before booking.

Location-based requests

You type your pickup and destination. The system assigns the nearest available rider automatically.

Upfront fare

The estimated fare is calculated when you submit the request — no surprises at the end of the trip.

No app required

Runs entirely in the browser. Works on desktop and mobile without installing anything.
